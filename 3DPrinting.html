3D Printing & Rapid Prototypes

Wearables, Props, and Custom Designs

About This Project

This collection showcases a variety of 3D printed projects, from functional wearables to artistic props and custom designs. Each piece represents an exploration of rapid prototyping techniques, material properties, and the intersection of digital fabrication with physical art and engineering.

The projects range from intricate wearable components for performance art to practical tools and decorative pieces. Using various 3D printing technologies including FDM and SLA, these prototypes demonstrate the versatility of additive manufacturing in creating both functional and aesthetic objects. Many of these designs are available on Printables for the maker community to remix and build upon.
