About Me

Leveraging a decade of experience as a Robotics Engineer, including my current work at the Georgia Tech Research Institute, I bridge the gap between academic research and public art through Performative Cyber-Physicality. My work transforms the city into a hybrid digital-physical playground. As ATLTVHEAD, I utilize custom-designed wearable robotics and IoT architecture to create a two-way interface between the internet and the real world. I adapt cutting edge technology to be human-focused, using engineering not just to solve problems, but to spark community, curiosity, and spontaneous joy!
